NA · NEW

And for Canadian manufacturers, the rules just changed

Health Canada expects cyber evidence with Class III/IV device licences and a change-control plan before your AI model learns anything new. The FDA refuses submissions without a credible cybersecurity file. CCSPA obligations are phasing in for designated operators. And if you export to Europe, the CRA and the new Machinery Regulation apply to you, not just to your EU competitors. Compliance stopped being someone else's specialty — it became the entry ticket to every market you sell into.

  • US & Canadian regulators aligned on premarket cyber evidence
  • One methodology, two regulators: FDA §515C / Health Canada PCCP
  • EU market access now runs through CRA + Machinery Regulation files
