AI-Act Conformity — one technical file, not two

For: makers of AI-enabled devices, vehicles, and machinery selling into the EU. The driver: the AI Act grafts onto your existing sectoral conformity assessment (MDR, type-approval, Machinery Regulation) — product-embedded AI due 2 Aug 2028, and a conformity programme runs 18–36 months.

  • AI-Act Art. 9 risk management merged into ISO 14971 / ISO 26262 / ISO 12100

AI Safety-Case Engineering

For: teams putting machine learning into safety functions. The driver: classical safety standards assume deterministic software — ML doesn't fit. A goal-based assurance case for a non-deterministic component is a functional-safety discipline, not data science.

  • ISO/PAS 8800 (automotive AI) & ANSI/UL 4600 (autonomous safety case)
  • EASA learning-assurance approaches for aviation ML
  • IEC 62304 + ISO 14971 + AI for medical software; ISO/IEC 42001 as the management layer

  • BMS & energy-storage functional safety (safety concept → assessment readiness)

  • Audits & assessments — ready at any time, across standards, products, and business areas
  • Capacity for sustainable compliance — enter new product, technology, or market areas faster

FSQ Experts integrates safety and security requirements at an early stage and in a holistic manner, so that risks, threats and interactions are systematically identified and managed.

  • Risk and threat analysis across the entire product lifecycle
  • Safety and security strategy — target state, needs analysis, governance
  • Innovative (semi-)automated safety & security analyses for complex, novel systems
  • Market monitoring and safety & security management
