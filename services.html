Our solutions

The five FSQ solution fields

Efficient Compliance

Compliance as an organisational capability

Effective compliance helps companies in highly regulated sectors to meet regulatory requirements efficiently, sustainably and across the entire organisation. FSQ Experts provides clarity on the current level of compliance maturity, identifies risks and establishes clear structures, roles and processes. Compliance is thus transformed from a reactive obligation into a manageable organisational capability.

  • Gap analysis of the organization & processes — full transparency into compliance maturity
  • Compliance strategy & roadmap — decisions based on robust analyses, not individual opinions
  • Audits & assessments — ready at any time, across standards, products, and business areas
  • Capacity for sustainable compliance — enter new product, technology, or market areas faster

Accelerated Market Access

Faster time to market

FSQ Experts helps companies in highly regulated industries efficiently bring safe and market-ready products to market. Whether you need modular individual packages for specific requirements or a comprehensive development partner, we bring proven safety and compliance expertise, structured processes, and field-tested testing and verification chains to your project.

  • QA & reviews of development results — early defect detection, lower audit risk
  • Practical support through the entire certification process
  • Approval & communication plan — coordinated regulator communication, stable approvals
  • Systems-engineering efficiency — end-to-end traceability, less rework, faster reviews

Adaptive Processes

Lean, scalable, future-proof

Adaptive processes enable companies in highly regulated industries to align their organizational, development, and security processes in a user-centric, flexible, efficient, and future-proof manner. FSQ Experts helps companies refine existing processes so that they reliably meet regulatory requirements while remaining scalable, lean, and adaptable.

  • Transformation strategy — clear priorities and a basis for management decisions
  • Process optimization and automation — less manual effort, fewer error sources
  • Process integration & continuous improvement across the product lifecycle
  • Resilient integrated management system — quality, safety, security, and compliance as one

Product Safety & Security

Resilience that endures

Product Safety & Security strengthens the resilience of organisations by ensuring that safety-critical products and systems are designed to be efficient, manageable, robust and compliant throughout their entire life cycle. FSQ Experts integrates safety and security requirements at an early stage and in a holistic manner, so that risks, threats and interactions are systematically identified and managed.

  • Risk and threat analysis across the entire product lifecycle
  • Safety and security strategy — target state, needs analysis, governance
  • Innovative (semi-)automated safety & security analyses for complex, novel systems
  • Market monitoring and safety & security management

Security in Systems Engineering

Systems of systems, under control

Operational safety remains stable even in the face of changes, new threats, and system expansions. Interaction risks are proactively identified and managed in a coordinated manner. FSQ Experts provides transparency regarding system boundaries, dependencies, and responsibilities, and develops integrated safety and security concepts across all involved systems.

  • Systems-of-systems architecture — boundaries, interfaces, security-related dependencies
  • Accountability & stakeholder management across organizations and supply chains
  • Coordination of safety demonstrations into a consistent overall analysis
  • Holistic security management at the system-of-systems level
