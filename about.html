Why our independence is structural, not a slogan

The big safety & security houses in North America are certification bodies that also sell consulting — and are therefore legally required to firewall the two, and barred from deep advisory work on anything they will later certify. FSQ Experts holds no certification-body accreditation by design: we are free to sit entirely on your side of the table, for any assessor you choose.

  • Structural independence — no assess-what-you-built conflict, ever
  • Named assessor credentials — our consultants' individual qualifications (assessor schemes, functional-safety certifications) are published, not anonymised
  • Verifiable firm certs — ISO 9001:2015 and TISAX, independently auditable

The market we serve from home

Medical devices: Ontario is the centre of gravity

  • Ontario: ~61% of Canadian med-device manufacturing value-add and ~58% of sector employment (~51,000 FTEs) [StatCan, unverified]
  • National sector: ~$22.6B operating revenue, ~3,360 enterprises [StatCan, unverified]
  • ~73% of Canada's med-device exports go to the US — every Ontario manufacturer faces FDA §524B [ISED, unverified]

Aerospace & rail: a real Toronto-region base

  • Toronto region: 160+ aerospace companies, 24,000+ professionals — Canada's second-largest aerospace cluster [Toronto Global, unverified]
  • Alstom: 5,000+ employees in Canada, three Ontario sites (Brampton, Kingston, Thunder Bay); Kingston hosts the country's only rail signalling test track [Alstom, unverified]
  • Ontario auto cluster: five OEM assemblers (Ford, GM, Honda, Stellantis, Toyota) [verified 3-0 in the 2026-06 strategy research]
