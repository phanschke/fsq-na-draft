## Cross-cutting regulatory drivers (the "why" behind most of the above)
- **EU Machinery Regulation (EU) 2023/1230 — applies 20 Jan 2027.** Drives ISO 12100, ISO 25119, ISO 18497, ISO 10218/TS 15066 harmonised-standard updates (adds autonomous/AI/self-evolving machinery + cyber requirements).
- **EU MDR/IVDR revision proposal — 16 Dec 2025.** Drives IEC 62304, IEC 60601-1, MDCG 2019-16 (cyber into GSPRs; CRA interplay). Still needs Parliament+Council (~12–24 mo).
- **EU AI Act harmonised standards (CEN-CENELEC JTC 21) — targeting ~Q4 2026.** Drives ISO/IEC 23894, 42001, and the TR 5469→TS 22440 normative move.
- **FAA aircraft cyber NPRM (§25.1319, FR 2024-17916).** Will make DO-326/356/355 family a mandatory AMC rather than special-conditions.
- **EU Rail Interoperability Directive (EU) 2016/797, mandate M/591.** Drives EN 50159/50155 revisions and IEC 63452.
