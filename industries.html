Railway

Safety · Quality · Certification

The railway sector is shaped by technological, regulatory, social, and organizational requirements. FSQ Experts supports companies in the railway industry in the key areas of reliability, availability, maintainability, safety & security, and testability (RAMST). Digitalization and AI are increasingly shaping the industry — from track monitoring to passenger recognition — while cybersecurity and cyber resilience are emerging as additional areas of focus.

What we deliver today
  • Functional safety per EN 50126, EN 50716, EN 50129 — concept phase to certification
  • Product cybersecurity per TS 50701 and IEC 62443 across the whole lifecycle
  • Safety analyses: RAM analysis, FMEA, FMEDA, FTA, TARA
  • Compliance & resilience: KRITIS, Cyber Resilience Act, ISO/IEC 27001
  • Delivered: EN 5012x safety & security concept for a battery management system; TÜV-assessed technical security concept with PMHF calculation

Aerospace

Safety first

Functional safety is critical in the aerospace industry. FSQ Experts assists aerospace OEMs with product development and compliance with stringent certification standards — development processes at aircraft and system level in accordance with ARP 4754A, software and hardware per DO-178C and DO-254, safety assessments per ARP 4761, and certification support across CS-23, CS-25, EASA Part 21, and SC-VTOL-01.

What we deliver today
  • Development processes: ARP 4754A system level, DO-178C / DO-254 software & hardware
  • Safety assessments per ARP 4761: PSSA, SSA, FHA, FTA, PRA, CMA, ZSA
  • Risk assessments for specific RPAS operations (SORA)
  • QMS & development assurance: ISO 9001, EN 9100, gap analyses, audit preparation
  • Delivered: 3-year avionics upgrade program — SSPP, system FHA/PSSA/SSA, supplier safety oversight
